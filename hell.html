About

"My personal Diary" was created especially for users who need an application,which allows to store the personals and other information and easily manage them. It is the best solution for organizing your business, Personal and also the private life secure.

  • The use of this Personal Diary is very simple.
  • It is very secured.
  • No one can access your Diary unless thay have your username and password.
  • You can create and save your notes.
  • Every note of your diary will be sorted according to the Time stamp.
  • It also has a delete option.
